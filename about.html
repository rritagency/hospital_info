About Us

Welcome to the Bangladesh Medical Information Center!

Founded by Zavier Huq, the Bangladesh Medical Information Center is a pioneering initiative dedicated to providing essential medical knowledge and resources to the people of Bangladesh. Launched on May 2nd, we are proud to be the first platform of its kind in the country, committed to empowering individuals with accurate information to enhance their well-being. At the Bangladesh Medical Information Center, we recognize the critical gap in accessible healthcare information in Bangladesh. With no other websites offering similar insights, more than 90% of the population lacks basic knowledge in the medical field. Consequently, even cases that can be easily controlled often escalate into critical situations due to a lack of understanding. Our platform aims to bridge this gap by providing comprehensive insights into common medical conditions, enabling individuals to understand their health better and make informed decisions.

Our platform offers content in both English and Bengali, ensuring that everyone can access education and information regardless of language barriers. Whether you're seeking guidance on common medical conditions, emergency procedures, or the latest medical research, our resources empower you to take control of your health journey.Furthermore, the Bangladesh Medical Information Center serves as a hub for medical education and awareness. Our collection of articles covers a diverse range of topics, from preventive care to advancements in medical research, fostering a culture of continuous learning and health literacy among our audience.

As part of our commitment to promoting health and well-being, we provide a comprehensive directory of hospitals across the country. Whether you're in need of immediate medical attention or seeking specialized care, our database offers detailed information on healthcare facilities, ensuring that you can access the services you require with ease. At the Bangladesh Medical Information Center, our mission is simple: to empower the people of Bangladesh with the knowledge and resources they need to lead healthier lives. Join us on this journey towards a healthier, more informed society.
